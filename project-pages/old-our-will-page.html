Our Will - Jötunheim
A narrative adventure with turn-based combat, about a young girl tasked with leading a small company of warriors, into an unknown and mystical land, in search of a cure to save their village.
Discription
This is a game about the lengths of human courage and will and the greyish line that most of the time separates good and bad decisions. It’s a game about the human relation with the divine and religion and how it perceives its role in the world. The game is set in a Scandinavian world, embedded deep in Norse mythology and magical beings.

The game is currently in development.

You can find the Itch.io page and GDD document here.

Combat System Concept
At an early stage in development, I was asked to create a unique Combat System for the game and pitch it to the team. Some ideas from this system ended up being incorporated into the current system we have now.

Since our game was gonna have turn-based combat, I looked at many different games from many different eras to try find an interesting an unique mechanic I could focus on.

I reached the conclusion that very few games allowed the player to controll the order of events in combat, so I came up with a system that did just that.
