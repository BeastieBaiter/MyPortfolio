P.I.G.E.O.N. - Personal Information Gathering Enhanced Operative Network
A interactive fiction game, where you play the role of a pigeon that is controlled by an oppressive, controlling government to spy on unaware civilians.
Company: Barking Beagle Studios
Platforms: PC
Accolades: First place winner, in the Games category of NTF 25 - Novos Talentos Fnac 2025
Team Size: ~5
Project Lenght: ~2 weeks
Key Responsibilities: Systems and UI/UX Programmer
Engine and Tools: Unity, Rider IDE, GitHub, ClickUp, Google Sheets and Docs
Game link: Itch.io
Project Background

P.I.G.E.O.N. was part of a group of games developed in the early days of my studio, Barking Beagle Studios. It was developed over the course of a week with the initial goal of testing team chemistry and development pipelines. It's a visual novel RPG where you play as a pigeon controlled by an oppressive government to spy on unsuspecting civilians. The overall experience was designed to be surreal and unsettling, creating a serious social commentary on the limits of government surveillance and its consequences.

Shortly after finishing it, we decided to submit it to a local Portuguese award contest, NTF 25 - Novos Talentos Fnac 2025, where we won first place and a prize of €5,000. This allowed us to develop the game further and release it on Itch.io using a "pay what you want" model.

Currently, we're working on creating a definitive edition of the game to be released on Steam.

Summary Responsibilities

I was one of two programmers involved in this game. I was responsible for programming several gameplay systems, including player stats, the core game loop, and most of the UI/UX.
