Hi! I’m João Pedro Pamplona
I’m an indie game developer who feels enjoyment and fulfilment in creating and designing video games and I’ve been making games since 2019.
Check out my games!
About Me
I took a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science Engineering at NOVA School of Science and Technology | FCT NOVA and I'm currently taking a masters degree in Computer Science with specializtion in videogames at Instituto Superior de Técnico.
I have also been actively involved in the videogame developer community, such as the youth association GameDev Técnico (GDT), where I’m currently developing Our Will, an indie story-driven turn-based RPG game, with the goal of launching it commercially. For this game I’m working as the Combat Gameplay Designer and UI programmer. I’m also part of the executive production team, where my duties entail finding, presenting, and producing interesting game jams for the members and creating/managing the teams that participate in those game jams.
