About Me

I took a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science Engineering at NOVA School of Science and Technology | FCT NOVA, and I'm currently taking a master’s degree in Computer Science with specialization in video games at Instituto Superior de Técnico.

I'm actively involved in the video game developer community, such as the youth association GameDev Técnico (GDT), where I’m part of the executive production team, where my duties entail finding, presenting, and producing interesting game jams for the members and creating/managing the teams that participate in those game jams.
I’m also currently developing, Our Will, an indie story-driven turn-based RPG game, with the goal of launching it commercially. For this game I’m working as the Combat Gameplay Designer and UI programmer.
